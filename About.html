About

As mentioned on the home page this wesbite is fundamentally a blog. We set it up as part of our Code First Girls course. We chose food as our blog topic as it is something we are both rather passionate about and as we both enjoy cooking and baking we could write about it relatively easily without much extra effort. We both have spent time looking at various food blogs ourselves and have taken inspiration from many of these when creating this. Our aim in this blog isn't to show one particular type of food but rather to show a variety, there will be some healthy recipes, some super treats, ideas for parties and having guests round and a mixture between both cooking and baking. We hope to deliver quality content to you and that you enjoy what the various recipes and posts that we provide. We thought it would be good to say a little bit about ourselves as well. Kenna is 21, studying for her postgraduate Masters in Data Science and is originally from a small town in Shropshire. She started taking interest in cooking at the age of 8 whilst on a family holiday to France. She helped her parents and grandparents with cooking the meals and has since carried on enjoying spending time in the kitchen. Faith ....
